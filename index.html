About SugarCrumb

At SugarCrumb, we believe every celebration deserves a handcrafted centrepiece. Founded by a duo of pastry lovers, our mission is to spread sweetness through thoughtful ingredients, sustainable packaging, and recipes passed down with a modern twist.

We source local dairy, organic flour and seasonal fruit whenever possible. From intimate cupcakes to grand celebration cakes, every order is made with care.
